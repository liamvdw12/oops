Whos 
mans?

My name is Liam van den Wildenberg I'm the handsome stock photo to your left. Basically, I've been obsessed with fashion and style for many years. It all started when my high school uniform had zero swag. I soon met a teacher who wore "funky" socks and I had found an easy way to break rules. I now had a style inspo, someone who was a good example of how to dress. My style journey has taken many paths since the ninth grade, from the ever so classic slim jean and Chelsea boot to my now wide pleated pant and loafers. But the entire way on my journey I've always loved making people feel good with fashion. Helping my homies who were less confident in the dressing department helped me realize how big of an impact your personal style has on how you feel. 

What I Do

So with what I just said, what's the point I'm getting at? What do I do as the subheading says? Well, I'm here to be your new go-to style guy! Basically meaning I'm here to style you or anyone you know who needs it. Whether it be overhauling your wardrobe, styling your models for a shoot, or it's just a special day and you need that one dope outfit to make you feel confident I'm your guy. I've worked a number of years in high-end retail keeping up with clientele and helping maintain their very own personal style. Throughout this time I've made it a goal of mine to make sure "No one leaves Liam's fitting room looking wack!" Hit the bookings tab and we can follow that rule together.
